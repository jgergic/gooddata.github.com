Welcome to GoodData. Let's get started.

35,000 reports computed last week
Setup

  • ➊ Sign up with GoodData

    Regular GoodData account will do. Fill out the form and you'll get a signup email in a couple minutes.

  • ➋ Create your Project

    Project is your database inside GoodData. You can have more then one project, but each project has separate users & permissions.

  • ➌ Download Dev Tools

    Depending on your preferred language/toolchain, download our Java CL tool or Ruby Gem
Build

  • ➊ Create Data Model

    Data Modeling helps us compute your reports.

  • ➋ Automate Data Upload

    Set up fully automated daily updates using our developer tools or API.

  • ➌ Create Dashboards & Embed

    Prepare vivid reports & dashboards.
Deploy

  • ➊ Auto-Provision Projects & Users

    Using our open APIs, link up your app with our infrastructure and provision new projects and users within seconds.

  • ➋ Manage Changes Across Projects

    Keep your users in sync with updates from your master template.
